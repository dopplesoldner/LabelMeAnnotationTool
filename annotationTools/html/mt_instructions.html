Amazon Mechanical Turk HIT instructions

The task is to draw bounding boxes around unlabeled vehicles and to add a category for the vehicle.

CATEGORIES: car, bus, pedestrian, cyclist, motorbike, lgv (light goods, e.g. van) and hgv (heavy goods, e.g. truck)


Please do not label previously labeled objects.

The following steps describe how to label an object:

1. Click the bounding box tool (rectangle) on the left toolbar
2. Draw a rectangle around a vehicle
3. A window will now appear asking for the object's name. Enter the object's name and click the "Done" button.
